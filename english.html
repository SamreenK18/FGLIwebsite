English Proficiency Tests


The four most common English language proficiency tests currently popular are- IELTS, DET, PTE and TOEFL. They are accepted by universities and institutions worldwide.

Each test has its own format, scoring system, and target audience. The best test for you depends on your specific needs and preferences. Consider the following factors:

Target Countries: Research which test is preferred by your desired universities or institutions.
Test Format: Consider your strengths and weaknesses in different test formats.
Test-Availability: Check the availability of test centers in your area.
Cost: Compare the fees of different tests.
